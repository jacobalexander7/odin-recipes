BBQ Chicken

Description:

This is a recipe for grilled chicken with BBQ sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Legs and Thighs
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Vegetables

Steps:

  1. Start grill with charcoal
  2. Lay chicken on grill
  3. Cook chicken until internal temparture reaches 165F
  4. Add vegetables to grill
  5. Apply BBQ sauce in last 1-2 minutes of cooking
  6. Serve chicken with vegetables on the side
