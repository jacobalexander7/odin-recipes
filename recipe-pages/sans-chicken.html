Sweet and Sour Chicken

Description:

This is a dish with baked chicken and a sweet and sour sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Sweet and Sour Sauce
  • White Rice

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Cook Chicken for 20-23 minutes.
  3. Check that internal temperature reaches at least 165F
  4. Remove chicken and toss with Sweet and Sour sauce
  5. Serve on a bed of white rice.
